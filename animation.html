Digital Animation

I created a 15-second animated video using Adobe Animate to demonstrate the principles of magnetism. The animation illustrates how same poles repel and opposite poles attract, with smooth motion tweens, labeled visuals, and transitions for clarity and engagement. I refined the magnet designs by adding distinct red and blue colors for the poles and incorporated text overlays, such as "Same Poles Repel" and "Opposite Poles Attract," to reinforce the concepts. To enhance aesthetics, I used a subtle yellow gradient background and added fade-in/out effects for seamless transitions.
